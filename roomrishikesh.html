Welcome to Rishikesh

Experience Divine Tranquility in Rishikesh

At Almost Heaven Rishikesh, immerse yourself in the spiritual pulse of the Ganges. Our homestay, nestled near sacred riverbanks or serene hillsides, blends boutique comfort with Rishikesh's vibrant energy. Wake to soothing chants, practice sunrise yoga by the river, or seek thrills with white-water rafting.

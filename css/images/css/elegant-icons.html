About Us

Almost Heaven Homestays

At Almost Heaven, we invite you to experience the magic of the Himalayas through our thoughtfully curated homestays in Mussoorie and Rishikesh.

More than just accommodations, we are a sanctuary where the warmth of a home meets the elegance of boutique luxury. Nestled amid mist-clad hills, serene valleys, and the spiritual energy of the Ganges, our properties are designed to let you unwind, reconnect with nature, and create memories that linger long after your stay.

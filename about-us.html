About Us

Home About Us

Our Mission

At Almost Heaven, we invite you to experience the magic of the Himalayas through our thoughtfully curated homestays in Mussoorie, and Rishikesh.

More than just accommodations, we are a sanctuary where the warmth of a home meets the elegance of boutique luxury.

Our Story

Founded by lifelong friends Gurpreet Khanna and Meeta Johar, Almost Heaven is a labor of love born from their shared passion for hospitality.

Together, they've created spaces where you don't just stay—you belong.

Our Philosophy

Nestled amid mist-clad hills, serene valleys, and the spiritual energy of the Ganges, our properties are designed to let you unwind, reconnect with nature, and create memories.

We believe in creating experiences, not just accommodations.
